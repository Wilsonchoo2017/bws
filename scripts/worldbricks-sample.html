Description

Escape to a charming retreat with the 31009 Small Cottage, a delightful addition to any LEGO collection. Released in 2013 as part of the Creator theme, this cozy abode boasts 271 pieces and features a single minifigure resident. With its rustic charm and whimsical details, this small but mighty cottage is perfect for display or imaginative play. The detailed interior and exterior design make it an excellent choice for LEGO enthusiasts and collectors alike, offering hours of nostalgic fun for those who remember it from their childhood, or a charming introduction to the world of LEGO brick-building.
